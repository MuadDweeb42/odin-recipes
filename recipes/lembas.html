Lembas Bread

"How many have you eaten?" "...four."

Almond, honey, and orange blossom mingle with vanilla in this soothing recipe that will leave you satisfied for days to come.

    Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 stick butter, softened
  • 1/2 c brown sugar
  • 1/3 c honey
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 T orange blossom water
  • 2 c AP flour
  • 1/2 c almond meal
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 1 T vanilla extract
  • 1/3 c sliced almonds
  • 6 banana leaves (for serving)
    Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Cream together the softened butter, sugar, and honey.
  3. Add the orange blossom water, vanilla, and lemon zest. Mix to combine.
  4. Add one egg at a time and scrape the batter down between additions.
  5. Add flour and almond meal in two to three additions. Scrape between each addition.
  6. Add almond slices and mix to combine.
  7. Wrap the dough in plastic. Keep it 1/2" thick and square to make it easier to cut later.
  8. Refrigerate for 30-40 minutes.
  9. Once firm, cut into 2" by 3" rectangles. Cut banana leaves to fit.
  10. Lightly grease a baking sheet. Arrange lembas on pan - they can be kept fairly close together because they won't spread as they bake.
  11. Bake for 20-30 minutes. Lembas should be firm and light golden in color. Cool before wrapping.
